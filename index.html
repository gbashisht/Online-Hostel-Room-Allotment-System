Hostel Facilities

Games

Our Hostel is inside of College Campus and all types of Games court and grounds are always open for hostel at any time. We have football court, Basketball Court Volleyball Court, Badminton Court,Cricket Ground Kabadi Ground and Floorball Court which encourges students to build thier carrier in games as well.

Library

Our college has a central library is is the largest library in the city which is also Open for hostel students even after the timming of college.

Gym

We have a special building for sports where one floor is alocated for gym runs daily for the hostel students we also have well trained Gym trainer and meditation room for the peace of Mind.

Free Wifi

Our hostel have free wifi and thier is one router installed in every floor of the hostel building which has 100 gb daily data limit for the hostel students to study well.

Hostel Tour

Our Hostels have all the facilities that a student need up to the mark level.

Hostel Rules

Before taking charge of room, you have to produce the receipt showing the payment of Hostel Rent and other fees. You also have to go for Medical Check-up at the Health Centre of the University before taking charge of your room. No part payment will be accepted.

  • Admission is open to full time students of Gwalior. Preference will be given to out-station students.
  • The application form completed in all respects should be submitted to the Office of the Chief Warden / Estate Officer / Deputy Registrar on any working day during office hours.
  • Admission to the Hostel will be canceled if incomplete or false information is furnished.
  • Admission will be valid for one academic year only. i.e. July to June, every year.
  • Daily attendance has to be marked in the Attendance Register during dinner time 7:30 pm – 9:45 pm. All residents must be present unless prior leave has been obtained from the concerned Hostel authority.

  • Fresh application will have to be filled up for next year accommodation
  • Students are required to give their consent about continuing the Hostel facilities in the next year, when asked by the authorities
  • Hostel fee for the next academic year is required to be paid in the month of June to July.
  • ITM University / ITM GOI, Gwalior reserves its right to cancel admission of undeserving students without giving any reason
  • ITM University / ITM GOI, Gwalior reserves its right to increase the hostel fee, if necessary.
  • ITM University / ITM GOI, Gwalior will not be responsible for any mishap.
  • Student residing in the Hostel managed by ITM University / ITM GOI, Gwalior shall strictly observe all the Rules and Regulations in force from time to time. Breach of rules / regulations may invite rustication / fine.
  • During their stay in the Hostel they will be under the control of the Officials of ITM University / ITM GOI, Gwalior/Rector.
  • Possession/ use of Alcoholic drinks, Narcotics, Drugs and Smoking is strictly prohibited in the Hostel premises. Severe disciplinary action including expulsion from the Hostel will be levied if any resident is found using or possessing these prohibited items
  • No guest or visitor of the inmate will be permitted to visit rooms. Visitors and guests should be received in the Visitors room or Reception area only between 9.00 a.m. to 7.00 p.m.
  • Every student shall be in his / her hostel by 08.00 p.m. If he / she has to stay out after the said timing owing to any special reason, he / she must obtain prior permission from the Chief Warden / Rector. The application for leave of absence from the hostel shall be made in writing thru dean to the Chief Warden and her permission shall be obtained. Late entry without prior permission will invite penalty / rustication
  • No students shall use the service of a hostel servant for personal work even on payment. He/she shall also not bring any servant from outside even temporarily.
  • Students will not enter rooms of other students without permission of the inmates. Students should not go to other students flat after 10.00 p.m. in the night.
  • Every case of illness and accident must be reported immediately to the Chief Warden/Warden.
  • No functions or celebrations shall be organized except with the permission of the Chief Warden.
